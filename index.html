EXPLORATION-HUB

newport oregon

A community hub dedicated to exploration and impact.

We envision NewLedo as a platform for pioneering ecologically kind projects and a destination filled with immersive inspiring experiences.

We are targeting Summer 2023 to be open to the public, inviting the community to engage with projects, share feedback and be part of a larger conversation regarding regenerative living.

In preparation for our summer opening, we are currently inviting farmers, inventors, makers, and local organizations who are focused on being responsive to climate and community.

COMMUNITY HUB

Emerging Strategy

Our vision is to have touchpoints across the site dedicated as spaces for experimentation. Resident and visiting inventors, makers and growers will be creating innovations in the areas of farming, energy, shelter and waste. Solutions will be installed in all areas of the farm including pastures and the dairy barn.


Our range of public focused activities will include immersive educational experiences, presentations, hands on workshops, and inventors’ demonstrations. Events, such as farm to table dinners, community gatherings, and cultural activities will be ongoing.


SHARE

Bring your project to the farm

Stay the night or longer. Our live /work program offers onsite housing and fabrication opportunities to regenerative practitioners and farmers working on early stage innovations in our four focus areas: farming, energy, shelter and waste.


LAND

NewLedo is an 18 acre farm on the Oregon coast situated between the Pacific Ocean and the Coast Range.

The site has wide open pastures, two creeks, a pond and wetland areas.


We’re currently engaged in restoration planning to create improved habitat for salmon and beavers. We plan to be open to the public, through tours focused on habitat restoration and watershed health.
